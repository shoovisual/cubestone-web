Privacy and policies

Cubestone Consulting ("We" or "Us") respects the privacy of our users ("you") and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information in connection with your use of our chatbot, Taxbot.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information from your interactions with the Chatbot:

  • Queries: The questions and requests you submit to the Chatbot.
  • Chatbot Responses: The information generated by the Chatbot in response to your queries.
  • Technical Information: Information about your device, such as your operating system, browser type, and IP address. This information is used for internal analytics and to improve the performance of the Chatbot.

2. Use of Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the Chatbot's functionality.
  • To personalize your experience with the Chatbot.
  • To analyze how the Chatbot is being used.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Disclosure of Information

  • To service providers who help us operate the Chatbot. These service providers are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential.
  • To comply with a legal requirement, such as a court order or subpoena.
  • To protect the rights and safety of ourselves or others.

4. Data Retention

We will retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

6. Your Choices

You can access and update your information by contacting us at taxbot@cubestoneconsulting.com

7. Children's Privacy

The Chatbot is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us attaxbot@cubestoneconsulting.com

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time by posting a revised version on the Chatbot interface. Your continued use of the Chatbot after the revised Privacy Policy is posted indicates your agreement to the new terms.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at taxbot@cubestoneconsulting.com.

Thank you for using the Taxbot!
