Overview

SBA Enterprise is an integrated solution to manage the external and internal activities of your business. It incorporates all types of transactions / functions which allows automating and growing your business. It is easy to bring the organization into a single system by using our SBA Enterprise Software Solution. Manage all the back-office and front office activities of your business and allow you to focus on your core business activities. Inventory management system(SBA Lite)

SBA Lite is a comprehensive software solution designed to empower small businesses, including shopkeepers, to manage their daily operations with ease. This user-friendly platform integrates essential modules for:

  • Sales Management: Streamline the sales process from quote creation to order fulfillment and invoicing. Track sales performance, manage customer accounts, and gain valuable insights to grow your revenue.

  • Purchase and Inventory Management: Optimize your inventory levels, streamline purchasing processes, and ensure you always have the right stock on hand. SBA Lite helps you avoid stockouts and optimize your cash flow.

  • Cash Management: Gain complete control over your finances with efficient cash management tools. Track income and expenses, manage accounts payable and receivable, and generate financial reports to ensure your business stays profitable.

Petrocard / Fuel Card Solution for Petroleum (With SBA / SAP BW Integrated)

Inventory Management
  • Branch / Station Wise Auto Price list
  • Credit Limit Check on real Time before proceeding the transaction
  • Company maintained Card Limits (Every individual company can maintain the cards limits which can be updated using web portal)
  • Invoice Posting Directly from POS system in Real Time
  • Customer Portal where Customer and individual cards can login and see the transactions
  • Dashboard for Customers
  • Drag and drop reports at runtime on customer portal
  • Individual column filter like Excel in Customer Portal
  • Web Transaction portal reports are exportable to Excel, Pdf
  • Individual cards can login and reset the pin
  • Tally template to upload data.
Customer Portal
  • Top-up by Quantity Or value
  • Auto Top-up (Monthly / Weekly)
  • Auto Top-up Balance Carry forward (enable / Disable)
  • Maximum Value Per Transaction
  • Customer Credit Request And approval
Customer Portal
  • POS devices will be linked to specific user so only configured device will be able to use the login (application cannot be used on another device until it is configured)
  • POS user cannot see the other station user’s transactions
  • While scanning the cards on POS only configured items in individual cards will display for transaction (ex one card is Configured to buy Diesel so he will not able to buy other products as it won't display other products on POS screen)
  • Station operator cannot make the transaction without the card holder input his secret pin
Transaction Management
  • Branch Wise Turnover
  • Card Balance Item Wise
  • Card Transaction with Running Balance
  • Card Wise Turnover
  • Company wise Turnover
  • Discount & Loyalty Earned Monthly
  • Father Customer Wise Status
  • Confidential Details Customers & Cards
POS Transaction Security
  • Customer / Card Master
  • Item Master
  • Price List Setup
  • Price Update
  • Upload Utility (Price / Auto Price Update)
  • User Authorization
  • Warehouse Authorization
  • Receipt and Payments (For Petrocard Customer)
  • Customer Credit Request & Approval
